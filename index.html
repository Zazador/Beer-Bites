Beer Bites Instructions

Beer Bites is perfect for the days when you don't know what to cook for yourself, but have a fridge full of food. To start, simply input the ingredients that would like to use from your refridgerator under the "Ingredients" header up above.

Beer Bites when then use this information to formulate a recipe that you can use to cook up these ingredients! On top of that, Beer Bites will recommend a beer that would pair well with the given recipe idea! Feel free to try any assortment of ingredients!
